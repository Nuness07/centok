Date: 2026-07-10

## Reset Rehearsal

1. Open `/app` with the demo account.
2. Use the reset action in the user menu.
3. Confirm the reset.
4. Verify available balance returns to 5.00 USDT.
5. Verify portfolio is empty.
6. Verify the default selected asset is AAPL.

## Main Demo Script

1. Open `/` and explain the simplified value proposition.
2. Click Get started.
3. Continue with the demo account.
4. Show supported Stock Tokens and select AAPL or NVDA.
5. Click Review order with the default 80.00 USDT amount.
6. Show insufficient funds and open Add funds.
7. Continue through BRL amount, review, mock PIX, pending, and success.
8. Continue back to the purchase review.
9. Confirm the purchase.
10. Show pending and success states.
11. Open Portfolio and confirm the new position.
12. Open Activity and confirm funding and purchase records.

## Presenter Notes

- Use the default scenario for the insufficient-funds path.
